Wolf

The gray wolf,also known as the timber wolf or western wolf.

  1. Like the red wolf, it is distinguished from other Canis species by its larger size and less pointed features, particularly on the ears and muzzle.
  2. Its winter fur is long and bushy and predominantly a mottled gray in color, although nearly pure white
  3. The gray wolf is typically an apex predator throughout its range, with only humans and tigers posing a serious threat to it.

Non-rabid wolves have attacked and killed people, mainly children, but this is rare, as wolves are relatively few
